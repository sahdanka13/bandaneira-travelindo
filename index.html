TOP 1

Banda Volcano

Apart from tourist attractions in the form of islands or buildings, another tourist attraction in Banda Naira is the Banda Volcano. Actually, the original name of this mountain is Mount Ganapus, and it is a volcano that is still active. On this volcano, you can find a variety of beautiful and interesting flora and fauna. Apart from that, the view from the top of this mountain is definitely adorable!

TOP 2

Hatta Island (Rozengain)

The next tourist attraction in Banda Naira which is no less charming is Hatta Island. This island, whose original name is Rozengain Island, has an underwater natural attraction which is definitely very popular with those of you who like activities in the water. The naming of this island as Hatta Island certainly cannot be separated from its history as a place of exile for Moh. Hatta or better known as Bung Hatta (Former Vice President of Indonesia).

TOP 3

Lava Flow

For fans of snorkeling activities, this snorkeling spot in Banda Naira will definitely make you want to stay for a long time. The story of its formation is a special attraction for those of you who visit here. Banda Volcano, which erupted in 1988, formed lava flows that flowed to the seabed. After some time, a beautiful underwater area finally naturally formed around the lava flow. This place is what many people know as Lava Flow.
